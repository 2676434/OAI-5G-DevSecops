1. Get the application name by running these commands:
  export RNIS-XAPP_POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "oai-rnis-xapp.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  export RNIS-XAPP_eth0_IP=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "oai-rnis-xapp.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[*].status.podIP}")
2. Dockerhub images of OpenAirInterface requires avx2 capabilities in the cpu and they are built for x86 architecture, tested on UBUNTU OS only.
